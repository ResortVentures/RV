Welcome to Resort Ventures of Lake LBJ

Resort Ventures of Lake LBJ is a resort management and real estate sales company located at beautiful Lake LBJ in the Texas Hill Country.  We are excited to announce the availability of two new spectacular co-ownership luxury vacation homes in the Clearwater Landing subdivision, located next to the Legends Golf Course on Lake LBJ.

With Resort Ventures you can co-own a luxurious vacation home to enjoy with your whole family, for only ¼ the cost and without the hassles of a whole home.  We offer financing of up to 75% of your purchase price with competitive rates, and help you to close on the home within days.  We also manage every aspect of the home from cleaning to billing, so the home is always ready for you to enjoy.

It's time for you to own a vacation home in Clearwater Landing, a place where you can build equity, keep family connections alive, and create a lifetime of memories for all generations to enjoy.  To learn more about our new luxury vacation homes in Clearwater Landing please click on the options below.
